---
title: "Syncing Calendar (Google / Microsoft)"
---

<Note>
  By syncing your calendar with Uninote, you can import schedules and utilize related features.
</Note>

This article outlines the steps to integrate with Google Calendar / Microsoft 365.

---

### Common: Open the Connection Screen

1. Open the [Calendar] screen.
2. Select [Connect].\
   <Frame>
     ![Screenshot 2026 02 26 22 07 58](/images/スクリーンショット-2026-02-26-22.07.58.png)
   </Frame>

---

- Sync Google Calendar
  1. Click [Connect] for Google Calendar.
  2. Log in with your Google account.
  3. When the access permission confirmation screen appears, grant the permissions.

---

- Sync Microsoft 365 Calendar
  1. Click [Connect] for Microsoft Calendar.\
     <Frame>
       ![Screenshot 2026 02 26 22 14 42](/images/スクリーンショット-2026-02-26-22.14.42.png)
     </Frame>
  2. Log in with your Microsoft account.
  3. If organizational approval is required, please request permission from your administrator.

---

### Adding Meetings Without Calendar Sync

<Note>
  Even if you do not use calendar integration, you can manually register from [＋New Meeting].
</Note>

1. Open the [Meeting] screen.
2. Click [＋New Meeting].\
   <Frame>
     ![Screenshot 2026 02 26 22 21 19](/images/スクリーンショット-2026-02-26-22.21.19.png)
   </Frame>
3. Enter the meeting information.
   - Meeting URL
   - Title
   - Save folder, tags
   - Date and time
   - Participants
4. Click [Create] to complete.
