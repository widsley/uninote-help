---
title: What is the maximum recording time?
---

<Tip>The maximum recording time per meeting by the recording Bot is **3 hours (180 minutes)**.</Tip>


## A. The maximum recording time is 3 hours (180 minutes).

After 3 hours, the recording Bot will automatically leave the meeting, and the recording will stop. The recording file up to that point will be automatically saved.

<Warning>Recordings longer than 3 hours are not possible. Please be cautious when recording long meetings.</Warning>
