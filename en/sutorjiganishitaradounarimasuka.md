---
title: What Happens When Storage Reaches Its Limit?
---

<Note>The behavior varies depending on the storage auto-add settings. By default, automatic billing will occur.</Note>


## A. Depending on the settings, either automatic billing or data write blocking will occur.

### When Auto-Add Setting is ON (Default)

* When the storage limit is reached, storage options to cover the shortfall will be **automatically purchased and added**.
* Additions are made in units of 100GB (¥20,000/month).

### When Auto-Add Setting is OFF

* The moment the storage limit is reached, data addition and uploads will be **systematically blocked**.
* It will enter a "Read-Only" state where only viewing, downloading, and deleting are possible.

***

### Pre-Alert Notifications

Alert emails will be sent progressively when the storage reaches the following percentages:

* **80%** reached
* **90%** reached
* **95%** reached

Additionally, pre-notification emails will be sent **one week** and **the day before** automatic billing occurs.
