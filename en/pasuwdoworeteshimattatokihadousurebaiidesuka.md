title: "What should I do if I forgot my password?"
---

<Tip>
  If you forget your password, you can set a new password from the reset screen.
</Tip>

## A. You can set a new password from the reset screen.

1. Click on [Forgot your password?] on the login screen.
   <Frame>
     ![Screenshot 2026 03 01 12 08 29](/images/スクリーンショット-2026-03-01-12.08.29.png)
   </Frame>
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. A 6-digit verification code will be sent to you, so enter the verification code and your new password.
   Subject: `Reset your Uninote password`  
   From: `no-reply@mail.uninote.ai`\
   <Frame>
     ![Screenshot 2026 03 01 12 10 12](/images/スクリーンショット-2026-03-01-12.10.12.png)
   </Frame>

---

### Additional Information

- If you do not receive the reset email, please check your spam folder.
- If you do not remember your registered email address, please contact your administrator.
