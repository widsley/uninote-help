---
title: Will downgrades and payment cycle changes be reflected immediately?
---

<Warning>No, downgrades and payment cycle changes are **not applied immediately**. They will be reflected as a "scheduled" change from the next renewal date.</Warning>


## A. Changes will be reflected from the next renewal date. They will not switch immediately after the operation.

### Applicable Operations

The following operations will all be in a "scheduled" state and will take effect on the next renewal date.

* Downgrading to a lower plan
* Changing the payment cycle (annual → monthly, or monthly → annual)

### Scheduled Change Display

* While scheduled, a "Cancel Change" button will be displayed in the admin panel.

### Restrictions During Scheduling

* While a change is scheduled, **no other plan changes or option changes are allowed**.
* If you wish to make another change, please first cancel the current schedule using "Cancel Change".

***

### Additional Information

* Upgrades (changes to a higher plan) are applied immediately.
* Changes that are applied immediately will incur prorated calculations.
