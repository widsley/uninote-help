---
title: "Registering Words in the Dictionary"
---

<Note>
  By registering words in the dictionary, proper nouns and internal terms are less likely to be distorted in transcriptions and summaries.
</Note>

---

### Steps

1. Open the [Editor] screen.
2. Select [Dictionary].
3. Click on [＋New Word].
   <Frame>
     ![Screenshot 2026 03 10 10 49 50](/images/スクリーンショット-2026-03-10-10.49.50.png)
   </Frame>
4. Enter the search word and replacement text.
   - Example: product names, department names, personal names, abbreviations
5. Click [Create] to finish.
