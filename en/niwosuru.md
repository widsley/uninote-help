---
title: Registering Words in the Dictionary
---

<Note>By registering words in the dictionary, proper nouns and internal terminology are less likely to be distorted in transcriptions and summaries.</Note>


***

### Steps

1. Open the [Editor] screen.
2. Select [Dictionary].
3. Click [＋New Word].<br>

    <figure><img src="/images/image (25).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
4. Enter the search word and replacement text.
   * Example: product names, department names, personal names, abbreviations
5. Click [Create] to complete.
