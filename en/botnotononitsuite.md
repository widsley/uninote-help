## Overview

To utilize the recording feature, the recording bot must be present in the meeting. This page explains how to invite the bot and the maximum recording duration.

## Steps

There are two methods to invite the bot to the meeting.

### Method 1: Use Calendar Integration (Automatic Entry)

1. First, [set up calendar integration](/ja/karendwosurugoogle-microsoft).
2. At the start time of the meeting, the bot will automatically attempt to join the meeting URL.
3. Click "Approve" displayed in the meeting to allow the bot to enter.

<Warning>If you do not click "Approve," the bot will not be able to enter. Please perform the approval action before entry.</Warning>


### Method 2: Manually Invite from the Meeting Page

1. Open [Meetings].
2. Click the [+ New Meeting] button.
3. Enter the meeting URL and press the "Create" button.
4. The bot will enter the meeting approximately 2 minutes later.

<Warning>It takes about 2 minutes for the bot to enter. Please perform the operation well in advance of the meeting start time.</Warning>


## Notes

* **The maximum recording time is 3 hours (180 minutes).** After 3 hours, the bot will automatically leave the meeting.
* When the bot leaves, the recording will stop. Recordings longer than 3 hours cannot be made.
* After 3 hours, the recording file up to that point will be automatically saved.

## Frequently Asked Questions / Troubleshooting

| Issue                        | Check                          | Action                               |
| --------------------------- | ----------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Bot does not enter (Method 1) | Did you click "Approve"?      | Please press the approve button.    |
| Bot is taking a long time to enter (Method 2) | Has it been more than 2 minutes since the operation? | Please wait about 2 minutes. If it takes longer, please try again. |
| Recording stopped midway     | Has it been less than 3 hours since the start? | The limit is 3 hours (180 minutes). |
