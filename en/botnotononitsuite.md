## Overview

To utilize the recording feature, the recording bot must be present in the meeting. This page explains how to have the bot join and the maximum recording time allowed.

## Procedure

There are two methods to have the bot join the meeting.

### Method 1: Use Calendar Integration (Automatic Joining)

1. Set up the [calendar integration](/ja/karendwosurugoogle-microsoft) in advance.
2. At the meeting start time, the bot will automatically attempt to join the meeting URL.
3. Click "Approve" displayed in the meeting to allow the bot to join.

<Warning>If you do not click "Approve," the bot will not be able to join. Please perform the approval operation before joining.</Warning>


### Method 2: Manually Join from the Meeting Page

1. Open [Meetings].
2. Click the [+ New Meeting] button.
3. Enter the meeting URL and press the "Create" button.
4. The bot will join the meeting approximately 2 minutes later.

<Warning>It takes about 2 minutes to join. Please perform the operation well in advance of the meeting start time.</Warning>


## Notes

* **The maximum recording time is 3 hours (180 minutes).** After 3 hours, the bot will automatically leave the meeting.
* When the bot leaves, the recording will stop. Recordings longer than 3 hours cannot be made.
* Once 3 hours have passed, the recording file up to that point will be automatically saved.

## Frequently Asked Questions / Troubleshooting

| Symptoms                         | Check                          | Action                              |
| ------------------------------- | ------------------------------ | ---------------------------------- |
| Bot does not join (Method 1)    | Did you click "Approve"?      | Please press the approval button.  |
| Bot is taking a long time to join (Method 2) | Has it been more than 2 minutes since the operation? | Please wait about 2 minutes. If it takes longer, please perform the operation again. |
| Recording stopped midway         | Has it been 3 hours since the start? | The limit is 3 hours (180 minutes). |
