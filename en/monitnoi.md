title: How to Use the Monitor Screen
---

## Overview

The monitor screen allows you to check ongoing meetings in real-time.

## Steps

1. Open **[Monitor]** from the menu on the left side of the screen.
2. A list of ongoing meetings will be displayed.
3. You can filter by participants, folders, tags, titles, and more.

## Notes

* To view or search past recordings, use the **Meeting Screen**.
