---
title: "Edit and Delete Folders"
---

<Note>
  You can edit folder names and members, as well as delete unnecessary folders.
</Note>

---

### Steps: Edit a Folder

1. Open the [Meetings] screen.
2. Hover over the folder you want to edit and click on the […] (menu).
3. Select [Edit].
4. Change the folder name or the folder members and update.

---

### Steps: Delete a Folder

<Warning>
  **Deleting a folder does not delete the meetings within it**. Meetings will remain in a "no folder" state.
</Warning>

1. Open the [Meetings] screen.
2. Hover over the folder you want to delete and click on the […] (menu).
3. Select [Delete] to complete the process.
