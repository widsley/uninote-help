title: Edit and Delete Folders
---

<Note>You can edit folder names and members, as well as delete unnecessary folders.</Note>


***

### Steps: Edit a Folder

1. Open the [Meetings] screen.
2. Hover over the folder you want to edit and click on the［…］(menu).<br />

    <figure><img src="/images/image (26).png" alt="" /><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
3. Select［Edit］.
4. Change the folder name or the folder members and update.<br />

    <figure><img src="/images/image (27).png" alt="" /><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

### Steps: Delete a Folder

<Warning>**Deleting a folder will not delete the meetings within it**. Meetings will remain in a "no folder" state.</Warning>


1. Open the [Meetings] screen.
2. Hover over the folder you want to delete and click on the［…］(menu).<br />

    <figure><img src="/images/image (28).png" alt="" /><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
3. Select［Delete］ to complete.
