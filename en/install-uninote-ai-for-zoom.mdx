## Overview

This page describes how to install the Uninote AI app from the Zoom App Marketplace and connect your Zoom account so Uninote AI can record, transcribe, and summarize your Zoom meetingsdescribes how to install the Uninote AI app from the Zoom App Marketplace and connect your Zoom account so Uninote AI can record, transcribe, and summarize your Zoom meetings.

## Step 1 — Install from the Zoom App Marketplace

1. Visit the [Zoom App Marketplace](https://marketplace.zoom.us) and search for **"Uninote AI"**.
2. Click **Add** to install the integration.
3. Authorize the connection. This grants Uninote AI the minimum permissions needed to join your meetings — see [Permissions & Bot Identifiability](/zoom-integration/zoom-permissions) for the full list.
4. Sign in to your [Uninote AI account](https://app.uninote.ai) to manage your settings.

## Step 2 — Connect your Zoom account inside Uninote AIStep 2 — Connect your Zoom account inside Uninote AI

1. Open Uninote AI and navigate to **Settings \> Integrations**.
2. Click **Connect Zoom**.
3. You will be redirected to Zoom's authorization page.
4. Review the requested permissions and click **Allow**.
5. You will be redirected back to Uninote AI with your Zoom account connected.

<Note>
  Multiple members of your organization can connect their own Zoom accounts independently. Each member's authorization is scoped to that member only.
</Note>
