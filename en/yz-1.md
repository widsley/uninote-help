---
title: "Add Users (Invite)"
---

<Note>
  There are three methods to add (invite) users.
</Note>

- Email invitation (for small groups)
- CSV bulk (to add multiple users at once)
- Invitation link (to distribute a participation link)

---

### Pre-check

- Choose an invitation method (Email invitation / CSV bulk / Invitation link)

---

### Common: Open the Invitation Screen

1. Open the [Users] screen.
2. Select [Add User].\
   <Frame>
     ![Image](/images/image-1.png)
   </Frame>

---

- Invite via Email
  1. Select [Send] and enter the email address of the invitee.
     <Frame>
       ![Screenshot 2026 02 26 21 18 44](/images/スクリーンショット-2026-02-26-21.18.44.png)
     </Frame>
  2. Set roles if necessary.
  3. Click [Invite] to complete.
     📧 An invitation email will be sent to the target user. If not received, please check the spam folder.
     Subject: `Invitation to join {Organization Name}`  Sender: `noreply@mail.uninote.ai`

---

- Invite via CSV
  1. Select [CSV].
  2. Obtain the template from [Download CSV Template] and create your file.
     <Frame>
       ![Screenshot 2026 02 26 21 16 54](/images/スクリーンショット-2026-02-26-21.16.54.png)
     </Frame>
  3. Upload the CSV.
  4. Click [Process CSV] to complete.

---

- Add via Invitation Link
  1. Select [Link].
     <Frame>
       ![Screenshot 2026 02 26 21 19 21](/images/スクリーンショット-2026-02-26-21.19.21.png)
     </Frame>
  2. Press [Copy] and share it with the intended recipients.

<Note>
  **Users who join via the invitation link will have the role of member by default.**
</Note>

If you want to change to a different role, please edit the role in the [Users] screen after the user has been activated.

---
