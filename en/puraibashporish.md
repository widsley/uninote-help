Hereinafter referred to as "the Company") establishes the following privacy policy (hereinafter referred to as "this Policy") regarding the handling of personal information related to the meeting recording, transcription, and AI analysis platform "Uninote" (hereinafter referred to as "the Service") provided by the Company.

The definitions of terms in this Policy follow the Act on the Protection of Personal Information (hereinafter referred to as "the Personal Information Protection Act") and related laws and regulations, as well as the Uninote Terms of Use.

***

## Compliance with Laws and Guidelines

The Company will handle personal information in accordance with the Personal Information Protection Act, other related laws and regulations, guidelines from the Personal Information Protection Commission, and this Policy, ensuring lawful and appropriate management.

***

## Acquisition of Personal Information

The Company will acquire personal information by lawful and appropriate means. The types of personal information that may be acquired in connection with the Service are as follows:

**1. User Authentication Information**

* Name and affiliation (company name)
* Email address
* User ID
* Password
* Information about individuals obtained through support or inquiries

**2. Recording, Video, and Call Data**

Audio and video data uploaded or recorded by users through the Service. This information is acquired and processed for the purpose of providing the Service's functionalities (such as transcription and AI analysis).

**3. Other Usage Information**

Service usage status, operation logs, access logs, device information (such as browser type, OS version, etc.).

***

## Purpose of Use of Personal Information

The Company will handle acquired personal information within the scope of the following purposes and will not use it for any other purposes unless there is consent from the individual or it is permitted by law.

1. Provision and operation of the Service (including transcription of recorded audio data and provision of AI analysis results)
2. Providing support and responding to inquiries related to the Service
3. Improvement and quality enhancement of the Service (including improving the accuracy of AI models and developing new features)
4. Billing, payment processing, and related administrative tasks for service fees
5. Incident response and security measures
6. Creation and analysis of statistical information (used in a form that does not identify specific individuals)
7. Providing information about new services and features from the Company
8. Matters related or ancillary to the above

***

## Provision of Personal Data to Third Parties

The Company will not provide acquired personal data to third parties (excluding business contractors) except in cases specified in this Policy, with prior consent from the individual, or as permitted by law.

***

## Delegation of Personal Data and Supervision of Delegates

The Company may delegate the handling of personal data to business contractors within the necessary scope to achieve the purposes of use. In such cases, the information delegated will be limited to the minimum necessary for the execution of the contracted work, and the Company will appropriately supervise the contractors.

***

## Voluntariness of Providing Personal Information

Providing personal information to the Company is voluntary. However, if personal information cannot be provided, the Company may not be able to provide all or part of the Service. Please be aware of this in advance.

***

## Security Management of Personal Information

The Company will take reasonable security management measures to prevent leakage, loss, or damage of acquired personal information. Additionally, necessary and appropriate supervision will be conducted for employees handling personal information.

***

## Disclosure, Correction, and Deletion of Personal Information

The Company will respond to the following requests from individuals or their agents regarding retained personal data.

* Disclosure of retained personal data
* Correction, addition, or deletion of content
* Suspension of use, deletion, or cessation of provision to third parties

**Request Method**

Requests for disclosure, etc., should be made via email to the contact information below. The Company will provide the designated application form. Submission of identification documents (such as a copy of a driver's license or passport) may be required.

In the case of requests by agents, a power of attorney and other documents designated by the Company will be required.

**In Cases of Non-Disclosure**

In the following cases, the Company may not be able to respond to requests for disclosure, etc. In such cases, the Company will inform the requester of the reason.

* If identity verification cannot be performed
* If the subject does not correspond to retained personal data
* If there is a risk of harming the rights and interests of the individual or a third party
* If there is a risk of significantly hindering the proper execution of the Company's business
* If responding would violate laws and regulations

***

## Deletion of Data

If a user terminates their use of the Service and deletes their account, or if the retention period specified by the Company has been exceeded, the Company will delete the user's data. Deleted data cannot be restored. For details, please refer to Article 18 of the Uninote Terms of Use.

***

## Use of Cookies and Similar Technologies

The Company may use cookies and other similar technologies to enhance the convenience of the Service and analyze usage status. Users can disable cookies in their browser settings; however, in such cases, some functionalities of the Service may not be available.

***

## Changes to the Privacy Policy

The Company may revise this Policy in response to changes in laws and regulations or modifications to the Service. Important changes will be announced in advance on the Service or the Company's website. The revised policy will take effect from the specified effective date.
