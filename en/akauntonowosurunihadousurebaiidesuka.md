title: How to Change Your Account Name?
---

<Tip>You can change your name at any time from the account settings. Changes are reflected immediately.</Tip>


## A. You can change it anytime from the account settings.

### Steps

1. Click on the account icon in the top right corner of the screen.
2. Open [My Account].<br />

    <figure><img src="/images/image (29).png" alt="" /><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
3. Click [Edit] and save.<br />

    <figure><img src="/images/image (30).png" alt="" /><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<Note>You can change your name at any time from the account settings. Changes are reflected immediately.</Note>
