title: "How can I change my account name?"
---

<Tip>
  You can change your name at any time from the account settings. Changes are reflected immediately.
</Tip>

## A. You can change it anytime from the account settings.

### Steps

1. Click on the account icon in the upper right corner of the screen.
2. Open [My Account].\
   <Frame>
     ![Screenshot 2026 02 28 22 09 20](/images/スクリーンショット-2026-02-28-22.09.20.png)
   </Frame>
3. Click [Edit], then press the [Save] button to save your changes.
   <Frame>
     ![Screenshot 2026 06 05 17 25 50](/images/スクリーンショット-2026-06-05-17.25.50.png)
   </Frame>
