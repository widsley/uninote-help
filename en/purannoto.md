## Overview

Uninote offers six plans: **Free, Lite, Team, Business, Pro, and Enterprise**. Each plan varies in storage capacity, data retention period, and support options.

<Note>The number of users is **unlimited** across all plans. There are no additional charges based on the number of users.</Note>

## Additional Information

* **Monthly payments are 1.2 times the price of annual payments**. Annual payments are more cost-effective for long-term use.
* If storage is insufficient, additional storage (100GB / ¥20,000/month・$140/month) can be purchased.
* Please contact us for details and pricing of the Enterprise plan.

<Warning>If you exceed your storage capacity, additional storage will be automatically purchased by default. If you do not wish to enable automatic purchases, you can turn it off in the settings (when OFF, it will become Read-Only upon reaching capacity).</Warning>
